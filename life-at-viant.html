Life At The Viant Data Engineering Team

"I enjoy the independence that comes with my work, as well as the challenges involved with working with Big Data."

- Maulik, Senior Software Engineer

Based at the Viant, inc. headquarters in Irvine, California, the Viant Data Engineering Team spends our time flying imaginary TIE Fighters, being tea aficionados, dodging nerf bullets (and sometimes nerf guns?!), going to Sam Woo a lot, and writing long sentences.

Just one of the many diagrams our engineers create to visualize the structure of the data we work with.

But sometimes, we do actual work. Building incredibly scalable distributed systems, gathering and analyzing huge data sets with over 1.2 billion records, and developing amazing products for clients is just a sample of what we do. We work in an agile environment that moves fast, but expect nothing less than top notch quality on all of our code, designs, and products. As a data marketing company, we also deal with loads of private and personal data and value honesty and integrity just as much as a brilliant engineering mind.

At an Angels vs. Oakland A's game during the summer.

We expect our members to be professional and have a solid work ethic, but we also like having fun. Company outings are scheduled periodically, so feel free to blow off some steam and mingle with other colleagues. Some of our outings include a trip to a game at the nearby Angel Stadium (tickets, open bar, lunch, and parking included!), and a team-building hiking trip at a trail.

"Innovation comes with freedom; here at Viant we get a lot of freedom to implement the projects in the most creative ways, giving our skill sets a big boost"

- Pratik, Senior Software Engineer

The team getting Indian food during one of our frequent lunches together.

As a member of our team, you will be valued and we guarantee that your efforts will have an impact. Transparency and clear expectations ensure that you’ll always know what your goal is. At all times, you’ll know what your teammates are working on, and vice versa. When the fruits of your labor reach the production release stage, everyone will know, because we have a gong you can ring to let everyone know about your success.

"Great group of people.

Yes, the selection of beers includes more than just Taiwan Beer.

Software development is hard work, but it doesn’t mean you have to be hungry or thirsty. Which is why we have coffee machines, pantries stocked with snacks, instant noodles, teas, as well as other items. For added convenience, the “Work-from-Home Fridays” program allows all employees to work from home on Fridays. Additionally. you’ll be granted free access to the gym located just a short distance away. If you’ve had a particularly exhausting week, even feel free to grab a beer from the selection in the fridge and join in on “Happy Hour” Thursdays/Fridays!

Just two LAMP developers taking a break from work.

If you're interested in joining our team, check out our Careers Page, or send an email to us at dataengineering@viantinc.com.

We strongly believe that our culture is one of the best in the software industry. We value innovation, integrity, and professionalism here (also humor, if you've got it). We work hard and play hard. If this is you, we like you already.
